# MSc Research Project

Title: [MICROWAVE ASSISTED COMBUSTION SYNTHESIS OF NICKEL VANADATE AND THEIR PHOTO CATALYTIC STUDIES]

Objective:
To investigate nickel vanadate nanoparticle via Microwave assisted combustion method and their photo catalyst performance for the degradation of malachite green/organic pollutant in sunlight/UV irradiation.
 

Experimental techniques used:

- XRD
- UV-DRS
- FT-IR
- Raman Spectra
- FE-SEM with EDX


Outcome:
NV2 > NV1 (i.e samples) due to their low band gap energy and smaller particle size and surface deformations.
